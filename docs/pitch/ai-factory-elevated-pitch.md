# Tejas AI — AI Factory Pitch

**Audience:** GPU halls, AI factories, edge colos, training environments.  
**Demo:** `/datacenter` + `/vr?enter=1`

---

## One line

> Tejas gives every AI factory a **digital twin testing ground** — train autonomous cooling, stress heat waves, repair in VR — graduate to the real hall with proof, not prayer.

---

## Three beats

1. **AI factories changed the rules** — rack power is a leading indicator; control in &lt;10s (Phaidra on NVIDIA). Everyone else still on BMS logic.

2. **You cannot learn on live GPUs** — one wrong setpoint = failed jobs. Tejas: twin → shadow → advise → control. Break chillers and heat waves in sim.

3. **VR repair = less downtime** — walk the hall, inspect GPU-16 exploded design, fix in AR — 25 min not 25 hr outage.

## 60-second script

> Phaidra runs cooling on NVIDIA's AI factories. Your hall has the same physics — 14 kW GPU racks, liquid loops, heat waves — but you can't let an unproven AI touch live training jobs.
>
> Tejas builds a full digital twin. Stress 47°C. Watch autonomous control beat baseline PUE 1.5 → 1.3. Flag GPU-16 months before trip. Walk the hall in VR. Fix the filter in AR.
>
> When you're ready: shadow mode on your real BMS, read-only, 30 days. Zero risk. Then you choose advise or control.
